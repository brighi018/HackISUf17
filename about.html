What We Do

Our goal is to provide a free, easy-to-use platform to help people struggling with mental illnesses to overcome their illness and live their lives to the fullest potential.

Mental health is a key part of overall health, and affects millions of people every day. If you are struggling with overcoming a mental illness, we want to be here as part of your support system, and to give you help along the way through personalized, constructive steps.

Who We Are

We are here to help.

We are a group of students who are passionate about helping people with mental illnesses, and raising awareness about mental health. But most importantly, we are here to help.
