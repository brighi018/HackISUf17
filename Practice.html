Practice

Build a path to help with mental illness. Select the activities you like the best and create a path to success.

Build Your Own Path

Select from the activities below to start your own path.

Go on a Vacation

Taking a trip can help you get out of stressful situations and allow you to relax.

Volunteer at an Animal Shelter

Working with animals can help relieve stress and also will help the animals stay happy.

Try Out A New Yoga Class

Practicing deep breathing and mindfullness can help your focus and to clear your mind.

Pop Bubble Wrap

Bubble wrap is fun to pop and also can clear your mind. Try using it whenever you get upset or need a distraction.

Build A Pillow Fort

Take some time to yourself and build a pillow fort. Watch Netflix or a classic Disney movie.

Declutter A Room

Cleaning out a room can improve your mind and help bring a new feeling.

Paint With Bob Ross

Watch and paint with Bob Ross. Add the new painting to your house.

Make A DIY Project

Take some time to yourself and make a diy project.
